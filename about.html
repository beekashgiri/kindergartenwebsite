Our Facility

We are two storey 10000 square foot very modern learning center in the downtown Toronto! Modern state-of-the-art facilities include 24/7 library with huge volume of material available online; making information access at fingertips. Computing infrastructure, with access to the very latest software, computer and wi-fi technology; giving students free and unlimited hook up to internet on the go from smartphones, tablet, laptop or personal computer.

Vision

We support and celebrate diversity by creating an environment that invites all cultures, races, religions, and expressions of gender and sexual orientation to be a part of our educational community.

We believe that responsibility is the foundation for students as they learn to become independent thinkers, doers and good citizens

We model collaboration through teamwork, communication, and partnerships with our families, educators and communities.
